CS DEPARTMENT INFORMATION

The Department of Computer Science at George Mason University is the largest in the College of Engineering and Computing, with nearly 80 full-time faculty members and over 2200 undergraduate and nearly 1200 graduate students. It offers a variety of degree programs, including Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. programs, with research strengths in all major areas of computing. The department is highly ranked, with a focus on cutting-edge research and interdisciplinary collaboration. Faculty excellence is reflected in numerous prestigious awards, including NSF CAREER and AFOSR Young Investigator awards, and includes several ACM and IEEE Fellows.

The department is committed to inclusiveness and accessibility, aligning with GMU's vision of increasing diversity in computing fields. It actively collaborates with industry, government, and educational institutions, with an External Advisory Board helping ensure programs remain relevant to workforce needs. Additionally, the department supports "Computing for All," offering diverse courses for students of all backgrounds, and encourages partnerships in research and education with alumni, local industries, and global institutions.
